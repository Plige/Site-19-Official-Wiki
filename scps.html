S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

Site-19 Roleplay has to offer a grand total of 11 different SCP objects to interact with at this time.

Given the nature of the game, it is uncommon to actually meet them without joining any official department first, but it is certain to happen anyway, if you give it enough time. These SCPs, you may find around the map, locked up in keycard rooms, or roaming around the facility if they've breached containment.

A careful examination of these objects and their re-containment procedures will be presented in each SCP's own section; you may head there clicking the links down below.

The mobile SCP objects:
SCP-173 The Sculpture

A sentient, extremely hostile, humanoid-looking moving statue: it moves as it is not seen.
SCP-096 The Shy Guy

A humanoid figure, passive at first, that when looked at in the face gets enraged and homicidal.
SCP-049 The Plague Doctor

A mysterious being with the looks of a medieval plague doctor; it seeks to cure the plague of life.
SCP-106 The Old Man

Humanoid, thought to have once been human, it is now able to teleport, and enjoys others' suffering.
SCP-999 The Tickle Monster

A blob-looking creature with pacific intent, wishing nothing but for the well-being of those around it.
SCP-682 The Hard-to-Destroy Reptile

An extremely hostile, large reptile that adapts to any situation with the sole intent of causing harm.

The stationary SCP objects:
SCP-008 The Zombie Plague

A prion disease that plagues those unfortunate enough to come into contact with it first-hand.
SCP-079 The Old AI

An old computer that has developed an intelligence of its own with time; it has now become evil.
SCP-914 The Clockworks Machine

A sentient machine capable of upgrading and downgrading items left into it through a panel on it.
SCP-012 A Bad Composition
SCP-1123 The Atrocity Skull
SCP-330 The Candy Bowl
SCP-1048 The Builder Bear
SCP-1162 The Hole in the Wall
SCP-178 The 3-D Specs
SCP-294 The Vending Machine
SCP-970 The Recursive Room
