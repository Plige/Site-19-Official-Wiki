S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay is a very important experience in its genre, as such, it requires a good organisation structure and good law.

Site-19 is structured the way a nation would: with smaller, autonomous bodies (the departments and the divisions) within a bigger complex that is the sum of them all. Essentially, Site Command are third in command with only the ESiD, Factotum and Administrator above them.

In order to achieve such high command ranks within Site-19, you must progress within the ranks, having joined the Roblox group and Discord server.

Fundamentals

This piece of Foundation Legislation implies that:

  • The Administrator is the highest authority within Site-19;
  • Their Factotum also shares the same authority;
  • The investigative body of Site-19, then called RAISA, which is currently disbanded, reported directly to them;
  • The Elected Site Director is the highest authority excluding XCOM;
  • The Assistant Directors (of Security, Research, Task Forces and Personnel) report to them and hold maximum power within their departments.

This piece of Foundation Legislation implies that:

  • The Site Director (also known as the Elected Site Director) is the head of roleplay affairs within Site-19, meaning they take care of it exclusively;
  • The Creative Director (also known as the Technical Site Director) is the head of development within Site-19, meaning they lead the developers team for it;
  • The Community Director (also known as the Assistant Director of Personnel) is the head of moderation within Site-19, meaning they lead the moderation team.
