S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

These are the departments that are most agreed upon to be "starter-friendly".

The Department of Security is the main group of armed combatants that work in order to maintain a safe environment for Foundation personnel deployed in Site-19. On the other hand, the Research & Containment department studies the anomalous objects in containment for better understanding of them and their special properties, that may be deemed too dangerous to keep around, or taken advantage of for development.

You may join any of these following our guide here!

Starter departments:

Department of Security

DoS

The Department of Security (DoS) is responsible for maintaining order and safety within Site-19. They are the primary armed force, tasked with protecting personnel and escorting test subjects to their designated locations. DoS members undergo rigorous training in combat tactics, weapon handling, and emergency response to effectively manage threats posed by SCPs and other hazards.

SUBDIVISION

Tactical Response Team

TRT

The Tactical Response Team (TRT) is a specialized unit within the Department of Security, trained to handle high-risk situations involving hostage situations and high priority VIP personnel handling. TRT members are equipped with advanced weaponry and tactical gear, allowing them to respond swiftly and effectively to emergencies. Their primary responsibilities include neutralizing threats, securing VIPs, and providing support during critical incidents.

Boosts: Speed, TK, HP (120/100).

Research & Containment

R&C

The Research & Containment (R&C) department is responsible for studying and managing the anomalous objects contained within Site-19. Their primary focus is on conducting research to better understand the properties and behaviors of SCPs, as well as developing effective containment procedures. R&C personnel work closely with other departments to ensure the safe handling and storage of these anomalies, while also exploring potential applications for their unique characteristics.
