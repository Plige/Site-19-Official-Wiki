S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

Site-19 has to offer three MTF task forces.

Each task force works independently, but will most likely be seen working in collaboration with the others, as they unite towards a common objective. The Nine-Tailed Fox, Hammer Down and the Red Right Hand are extremely specialized in their own line of work, so there is no clear gerarchy!

You may join the Mobile Task Forces following our guide, here!

Mobile Task Forces:

Epsilon-11 "Nine-Tailed Fox"

E-11

The Nine-Tailed Fox is Site-19's primary Mobile Task Force, specializing in rapid response to containment breaches and other emergencies. They are trained to handle a wide range of situations, from securing dangerous SCPs to evacuating personnel and civilians. The Nine-Tailed Fox is known for their versatility and adaptability, making them a crucial asset in maintaining the safety and security of Site-19.

Boosts: Speed, HP (120/100).

Nu-7 "Hammer Down"

Nu-7

Hammer Down is Site-19's elite combative Mobile Task Force, specializing in heavy assault and Insurgent suppression operations. They are equipped with advanced weaponry and armor, allowing them to take on the most dangerous incursions and hostile threats. Hammer Down is often deployed in high-risk situations where brute force and overwhelming firepower are required to achieve mission objectives. Their reputation for effectiveness and fearlessness makes them a formidable presence within Site-19.

Boosts: Speed, HP (150/100).

Alpha-1 "Red Right Hand"

A-1

The Red Right Hand is Site-19's covert operations Mobile Task Force, specializing in clandestine missions and high-risk extractions. They operate in the shadows, often undertaking assignments that require stealth, precision, and discretion. The Red Right Hand is trained to handle sensitive situations, such as retrieving escaped SCPs, high-risk, complex hostage scenarios or neutralizing threats without drawing attention. Their expertise in covert tactics and their ability to operate undetected make them an invaluable asset to Site-19's overall security strategy.

Boosts: NVG, Speed, TK, HP (150/100).
