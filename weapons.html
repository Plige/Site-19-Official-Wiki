S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

Site-19 Roleplay presents the user several different weapons, many of which are team-locked.

Site-19 has a lot to offer when it comes to weapons: submachine guns, light machine guns, heavy machine guns, pistols, melees and even a sniper rifle. All these but those no longer in use may be obtained either by looking for them in the on-site armouries, or by joining a specific team. Most of these weapons are unobtainable, meaning that the only way to get them it to spawn with them, therefore requiring the player to be apart of an department or division.

In order to unlock an exclusive department to use its exclusive weapons, one must join an official department. Here's how.
