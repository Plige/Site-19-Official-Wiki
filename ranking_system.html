S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay is a Roblox experience that focuses entirely on the RP aspect of it.

This means that unlocking exclusive teams and divisions and engage in the roleplay to a deeper level than that normally accessible, the doors open to the player revealing much more possibilities in fun activities and engagement with the community, including private events hosted with the highest ranking members of the S.C.P. Foundation itself. To unlock such things, one must join the official ranks, and here is how.

You may achieve the first official rank by joining the Roblox group and Discord server.

Entry ranks:

Guest

Guest

Guest is the rank given to new players who have not joined the official S.C.P. Site-19 Roplay Roblox group.

They possess very limited access to community trainings, events, and are unable to join official departments and divisions.

Level-0

L-0

Level-0 is the rank given to new players who have joined the official S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay Roblxo group.

They currently possess little access to community trainings or events, but are eligible to progress further into the ranks by filling out a form.

They are to learn the contents of the public Foundation Legislation.

Awaiting Orientation

AO

Awaiting Orientation is the rank given to players who have succesfully answered all questions in the AO Application, found in the main Discord server.

They now have basic access to community trainings and events, and are eligible to join official departments and divisions via tryout or application.

Departmental ranks:

ENLISTED PERSONNEL

Level-1

L-1

Level-1 is the rank given to players who have joined an official department or division.

They now have access to department-specific trainings, events, and team, as well as the ability to progress further into Foundation ranks.

Level-1 personnel includes R&C Interns, DoS Cadets & Privates, and MTF Trainees.

Level-2

L-2

Level-2 is the rank given to players who have shown dedication and commitment to their department or division.

They now have increased authority within their department and may progress further into department ranks, approaching Medium Command positions.

Level-2 is also the entry rank for the elite divisions: the Tactical Response Team and SMTF Alpha-1.

MEDIUM COMMAND

Level-3

L-3

Level-3 is the rank given to players who have attained a Medium Command position within their department or division.

They now have significant authority and responsibility, overseeing enlisted personnel and ensuring the smooth operation of their department.

Level-3 personnel host departmental trainings and events for lower-ranked members.

HIGH COMMAND

Level-4

L-4

Level-4 is the rank given to players who have attained a High Command position within their department or division.

They now hold top-tier authority, making critical decisions that impact the whole department and its operations.

Level-4 personnel host department-wide trainings, events, and recruit new members into the division they're apart of.

Facility Senior Staff

FSS

Facility Senior Staff is the rank given to players who have reached the pinnacle of their department or division.

They are entrusted with overseeing the entire department's operations and ensuring alignment with Foundation goals.

In the Mobile Task Forces, they are the Task Force Commanders.

They are the department's Second-in-Command.

Site Command

SiC

Site Command is the highest rank attainable within a department or division.

They hold ultimate authority over their department's operations and are responsible for strategic decision-making that shapes the department's future.

They are the department's First-in-Command.

Elected Site Director

Elected Site Director

ESiD

Elected Site Director is the rank obtained through the democratic vote set to choose the best out of several candidates.

They are in charge of keeping Site-19 running, ensuring the cooperation of all the departments and divisions of it, at once.

They are the highest authority normally achievable within Site-19, and are controlled by the Elections Committee.

EXECUTIVE COMMAND

Factotum

Factotum

Factotum is the rank given to the members that have shown extreme levels of competence and administrative skills.

They are appointed by The Administration himself.

They oversee the entirety of Site-19, ensuring coordination and efficiency across the Foundation's operations.

Currently, there is only one Factotum in Site-19.

O5 Council

O5

The O5 Council is the highest authority within the S.C.P. Foundation.

They oversee all Foundation operations globally, making critical decisions that impact the entire organization.

They possess unparalleled authority and are responsible for maintaining the secrecy and security of the Foundation's activities.

The O5 Council no longer exists, having been replaced by the Site Command.
