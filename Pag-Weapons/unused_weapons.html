Unused weapons:

PRIMARY WEAPONS

LMG FN Minimi / M249 SAW

A light machine gun manufactured by FN Herstal. It is used by various Foundation units for its high rate of fire and sustained suppressive capabilities in combat situations.

  • Damage points: 33 per body shot; 66 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 200 rounds per magazine.
SNIPER CheyTac M200 Intervention

A bolt-action sniper rifle manufactured by CheyTac LLC. It is utilized by specialized Foundation marksmen for long-range engagements and precision targeting of high-value threats.

  • Damage points: 130 per body shot; 260 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 6 rounds per magazine.

SECONDARY WEAPONS

PISTOL Beretta M9 (silenced)

A silenced semi-automatic pistol manufactured by Beretta. It is commonly issued to Foundation personnel for self-defense and covert operations where discretion is required.

  • Damage points: 25 per body shot; 50 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 22 rounds per magazine.
