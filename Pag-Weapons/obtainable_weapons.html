Obtainable weapons:

PRIMARY WEAPONS

PDW FN P90

A compact personal defense weapon manufactured by FN Herstal. It is used by various Foundation personnel, including Mobile Task Force units and Security teams, due to its high rate of fire and armor-piercing capabilities.

  • Damage points: 14 per body shot; 28 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 50 rounds per magazine.
PDW AAC Honey Badger

A compact personal defense weapon developed by Advanced Armament Corporation (AAC). It is favored by certain Foundation units for its versatility, suppressor integration, and effectiveness in close-quarters combat.

  • Damage points: 19 per body shot; 38 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 30 rounds per magazine.
AR U.S. Colt Model 16 - M16

A standard-issue assault rifle used by various Foundation personnel, including Security teams and Mobile Task Force units. Known for its reliability, accuracy, and versatility in combat situations.

  • Damage points: 20 per body shot; 40 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 30 rounds per magazine.
SMG KRISS Vector

A submachine gun developed by KRISS USA, known for its unique recoil mitigation system. It is utilized by certain Foundation units for its compact size, high rate of fire, and effectiveness in close-quarters engagements.

  • Damage points: 18 per body shot; 36 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 25 rounds per magazine.

SECONDARY WEAPONS

PISTOL Glock 22

A reliable and widely used semi-automatic pistol chambered in .40 S&W. It is standard issue for many Foundation personnel, including Security teams and Mobile Task Force units, due to its durability and ease of use.

  • Damage points: 11 per body shot; 22 per headshot.
  • Ammo capacity: 10 rounds per magazine.

MELEES

MELEE Kitchen Knife

A standard kitchen knife used by Foundation personnel for close-quarters combat and utility purposes. It is a versatile melee weapon favored for its sharpness and ease of handling.

  • Damage points: 10 per hit.
MELEE Wrench

A sturdy wrench used by Foundation personnel for both maintenance tasks and as a makeshift melee weapon. It is valued for its durability and effectiveness in close combat situations.

  • Damage points: 5 per hit.
