S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay has to offer immersive RP to all official members.

These are the players belonging to any of Site-19's many official departments and divisions to choose from. They range from entirely RP experiences, to combative units. Amongst these are three units of the SCP Mobile Task Forces, an official Research & Containment department, and the Department of official Security.

Players belonging to any of these departments, or any of the yet to mention ones, will have access to an exclusive team morph in-game.

You may join any of these below following the guide we've made for you!

Active Departments:
DoS Department of Security

The main line of defense of Site-19; they also carry out daily on-site operations.

R&C Research & Containment

The field experts in containment and experimentation on contained SCP objects.

MTF Mobile Task Forces

The most specialized operators of the SCP Foundation: Epsilon-11, Nu-7, Alpha-1.

ADMINISTRATIVE DEPARTMENTS

AdO Administrations Office

The Site-19 moderators, who enforce regulations in-game and on the discord servers. They also host events and handle ban, blacklist and warning appeals, along with foundation personnel reports, through the Universal Response Form.

ELC Elections Committee

They are in charge of handling Site Director elections, ensuring a fair and regular competition amongst the competing candidates.
