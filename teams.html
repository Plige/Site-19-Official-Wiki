S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

Site-19 Roleplay has several unique teams to choose from, and many more that are now unused or locked to most players.

In S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay, you (the player) get to choose what to RP as. You may choose to become apart of the security protecting Foundation personnel, a scientist researching the anomalies in containment, a D-Class awaiting testing or an ethics committee member protecting the lives of the test subjects from unethical treatment.

In order to unlock exclusive teams to play as, you must join an official department or buy a gamepass.

Foundation Teams:

Security Department

SD

The main line of security on-site, to prevent the breach of contained SCP objects, the escape and revolt of test subjects, and to combat the mercenaries belonging to the C.I. before they have a chance to hit essential Foundation assets with their attacks.

Players on the Security Department team on-site may choose to fight as normal soldiers or as combat medics, using the locker found in their spawn armory, in the EZ.

Research & Engineering R&E

These are the experts who study the anomalous objects in containment.
Ethics Committee EC

These are who protect every test subject's right, maintaining ethics.
Medic MD

These are the on-site medics, who will cure the injured when necessary.
Janitor JD

These are very skilled people apt for the cleaning of anomalous agents.

GAMEPASS TEAMS

MTF Beta-7 "The Maz Hatters" B-7

Operatives of the Mobile Task Forces specialized in the containment of bio-hazardous SCP objects such as SCP-008 and its instances.
They also have a health boost: 120/100.

Price: 250R$

Intelligence Agency IA

Field operators of an independent Intelligence Agency, that witness and oversee the operations of the SCP Foundation first-hand.

Price: 428R$

Hostile/Neutral Teams:

Class-D CD

Death row inmates that have signed a contract to receive a pardon for their crimes, in exchange for their assistance in the research of contained anomalies as test subjects, should they conclude their contract with the Foundation.
