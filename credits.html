He is the Founder of S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay, but is no longer involved in the development of the game. He also created all the original Roblox groups linked to the game, and has stopped managing those, too.

ThunderGemios10 is The Administrator of Site-19: his is the highest rank excluding AdministratorGnar's, which gives him Administrator permissions within the Discord servers he's in.
