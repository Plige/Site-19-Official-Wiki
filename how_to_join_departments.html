S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay has to offer many official departments and divisions of various kinds and different objectives, mostly being roleplay-related.

One may join these departments and divisions/s by obtaining the rank of Awaiting Orientation and following the unit-specific guides below.

You may obtain the rank of Awaiting Orientation by filling out the AO Application, found in the Discord server!

Department of Security DoS
The Department of Security is a beginner-friendly official combative department within Site-19. The entry rank is Security Cadet. Its current director is user Noobmaster12836.

How to join the Department of Security?

In order to join the Department of Security, one must:

  1. Wait for the DoS applications to open;
  2. Join the DoS Discord server;
  3. Verify their Roblox account in there, if necessary;
  4. Read all DoS documentation and regulations of public access;
  5. Fill in the form found in the applications channel;
  6. Submit the application containing all correct answers, information, and having used proper grammar throughout;
  7. Wait for the application results to be revealed in the app results spreadsheet, that is also found in the applications channel.
Tactical Response Team TRT
The Tactical Response Team is a highly specialized official combative & rapid response unit within Site-19. It is a subdivision of the DoS, and the entry rank is Tactical Response Cadet. Its current Captain is user thefox11withthemtf.

How to join the Tactical Response Team?

In order to join the Tactical Response Team, one must:

  1. Join the Department of Security;
  2. Obtain a rank of Security Lance Corporal or above within the DoS;
  3. Wait for a Tactical Response Team Internal Observational Tryout (TRT IOT) to be hosted;
  4. Attend and succeed throughout the IOT, in both its practical and off-site parts;
  5. Wait for your results to be reported to you by TRT High Command.
