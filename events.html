S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

In S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay, a user may partake in many different kinds of community events.

Considering the following suggested community events, some revolve around Specialized Teams' work, such as SMTF E-11 "Nine Tailed Fox" involvement in Breach Events, or AMTF Nu-7 "Hammer Down"'s in Riot Events, while others, like Keter Call Saul, most External Operations, Test Events and Hide & Seek Events, let everybody join and partake in the activities.

You may join any of the following by joining the Discord server

Artificial Events:

Breach Event - BE

During a Breach Event the primary lighting system fails, leaving the facility in darkness, and all SCPs in containment breach, putting at risk the life of all personnel in the facility. During such event it is advised to stay in the Evacuation Shelter until MTF have captured all breached SCP objects.

The main support during a BE is MTF Epsilon-11 "Nine-Tailed Fox", but AMTF Nu-7 "Hammer Down" may also assist with the containment of SCP-682 on the surface.

Riot Event - RE

During a Riot Event, the lights go out, and the door to the Light Containment Zone Weapon Supply blows open. As such, all the test subjects arm themselves and attempt to fight their way out of the facility. During a Riot Event, all combative units are advised to hold the checkpoints to the Entrance Zone or head to the supply area, while non combatives keep themselves in the Evacuation Shelter.

The main support during a RE is AMTF Nu-7 "Hammer Down".

Casual Events:

Keter Call Saul KCS

Often hosted by the ESiD, the KCS event is an experience of tribunal roleplay, where there are a defendant and a plaintiff.

External Operation EO

External Operations are roleplay expeditions to other Roblox experiences, often related to science-fiction like Site-19 itself.

Foundation Seminar

Foundation Seminars are events in which the Elected Site Director receives personnel and answers their questions.

Test Event

Hosted by R&C HRs, Test Events are tests for the public to witness and engage in.
