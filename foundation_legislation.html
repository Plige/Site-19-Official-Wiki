S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay is a very important experience in its genre, as such, it requires a good organisation structure and good law.

Site-19 presents an enourmous amount of documents that regulate the activity of every and each member of foundation personnel or simply any community member. In addition to those, are the pieces of legistation that ensure that every member has equal rights and maintains respect towards each other.

To ensure that you are always updated on any legislative change, or any other change too for that matter, you may join the Discord server!

Fundamentals
FR Facility Regulations

This is one of the oldest pieces of legistation in Site-19 and it is often thought of as the most important one.
It explicitly states what one may or may not do while on-site, including item (loadout or gear) and area restrictions.

FO Foundation Overview

The Foundation Overview aims to provide a general understanding of Site-19 and its most main aspects, including the description of the many departments and their ranks, faces & formations exercises' lecture and a few response protocols.

CoC Code of Conduct

The Code of Conduct is an extremely important document that dictates the rights and duties of every member of personnel. As such, it explains and lists every crime a member might be accused of, and their punishments.
This includes those of external influence, such as asset-stealing.
