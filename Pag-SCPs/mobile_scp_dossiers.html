The mobile SCP objects

SCP-173 The Sculpture

A hostile, animate statue that can only move when not being observed. It is known for its extreme speed and lethality, making it one of the most dangerous SCPs contained by the Foundation.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

  1. Always have somebody watching SCP-173 as long as you are exposed to its attacks: either have two people or more looking at the same time or get behind cover to blink;
  2. Shoot at it, preferably, in the head.

BE method (MTF E-11 only):

  1. Deploy first once the BE has started in order to obtain the Containment Box;
  2. Approach SCP-173;
  3. Cautiously get close to it until it enters the Containment Box;
  4. Take it back to its inner containment chamber.

NOTICE!

During Breach Events, if SCP-079 is breached, SCP-173 will be able to attack nearby players even when it is being looked at, moving freely for about 4 seconds once every 30 seconds. As such, unless you are a trained E-11 Operator with the Containment Box, you are to avoid getting close to SCP-173 until SCP-079 is re-contained.

SCP-096 The Shy Guy

A humanoid creature that becomes extremely agitated and violent when its face is viewed, whether directly, via video recording, or even in photographs. Once its face has been seen, SCP-096 will relentlessly pursue and kill the viewer.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

IF YOU ARE ON PC:

  1. Position yourself in front of SCP-096's when it is not enraged, making sure to look the opposite way;
  2. Shoot at it in third person view, preferably in the head.

IF YOU ARE ON MOBILE:

  1. Carefully move around SCP-096 hugging the walls and looking at them;
  2. Once you're behind SCP-096, turn around and shoot at it, preferably in the head.

Luring method:

  1. Look at SCP-096's face, then begin moving to SCP-096's inner containment chamber;
  2. Quickly enter its containment box;
  3. Wait as SCP-096 arrives, then accept demise while standing still;
  4. Have somebody close SCP-096's containment box using the lever in its control room.
SCP-049 The Plague Doctor

A humanoid entity dressed in the attire of a medieval plague doctor. SCP-049 is capable of speech and demonstrates a high level of intelligence. It believes itself to be a healer and will attempt to "cure" individuals it deems to be afflicted with a "pestilence" by performing surgery, which invariably results in the death of the subject.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

  1. Have SCP-049 follow you;
  2. Position yourself on top of any block that SCP-049 is incapable of climbing (EG. A table; A wall);
  3. Have SCP-049 stick to you while being out of its reach;
  4. Shoot at it, preferably in the head.
SCP-106 The Old Man

A malevolent entity that appears to be an elderly humanoid. SCP-106 is capable of passing through solid matter and can corrode materials it comes into contact with. It is known for its ability to create a pocket dimension, referred to as its "pocket space", where it traps victims before eventually releasing them, often in a severely deteriorated state.

Re-containment procedure?

Recall method:

  1. Head to its control room, in Heavy Containment Zone;
  2. Make sure the Elo-IID Magnets lever points towards "OFF";
  3. Insert the first (1 of 2) Level-5 keycard into any keycard reader next to the console;
  4. Enable Sound transmission;
  5. Enable the femur breaker;
  6. Wait for SCP-106 to come out of the ground under the bridge between the entrance to its containment chamber and its control room;
  7. Insert the second (2 of 2) Level-5 keycard into the other keycard reader;
  8. Immediately enable the Elo-IID Magnets;
  9. Disable Sound transmission.
SCP-999 The Tickle Monster

A friendly, gelatinous entity known for its playful and affectionate nature. SCP-999 is capable of inducing feelings of happiness and euphoria in those it interacts with, often through physical contact. It is considered one of the least dangerous SCPs and is often used in therapeutic settings to alleviate depression and anxiety among Foundation personnel.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

  1. Shoot at it.
SCP-682 The Hard-to-Destroy Reptile

A highly intelligent and extremely hostile reptilian creature known for its incredible regenerative abilities and adaptability. SCP-682 has a deep-seated hatred for all life forms and will go to great lengths to destroy anything it perceives as a threat. Due to its resilience and aggression, SCP-682 is considered one of the most dangerous SCPs contained by the Foundation.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

  1. Shoot at it;
  2. A lot.
