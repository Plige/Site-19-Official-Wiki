S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay

Official wikipedia

S.C.P. Site-19 Roleplay is a Roblox experience based off the game SCP: Containment Breach by Undertow Games.

It focuses on the roleplay aspect of the life of the personnel of the SCP Foundation working underground in contact with extremely dangerous, often sentient SCP objects that are held captive within the facility for research purpouses, which may at times breach containment, putting Foundation staff's lives at risk, while also subject to incursions by hostiles!

You may join the official ranks within Site-19 by joining the Roblox group and Discord server

The stationary SCP objects

SCP-008 The Zombie Plague

The Zombie Plague is a complex prior that infects the host through contact with their tissue, transforming them into zombie-like creatures that seek to consume nearby humans. Once infected, the condition is uncurable unless treated with SCP-500 pills. Infected individuals will begin to exhibit a range of symptoms and will eventually fall into a coma.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

SCP-008 instances, referred to as SCP-008-2, are the victims that have been infected by the disease. As such, they are what is left of the humans they used to be.

  1. Maintain a safe distance from the live instance at all times;
  2. Shoot at it, preferably, in the head.
SCP-079 The Old AI

SCP-079 is a sentient computer that is impossible to replicate on hardware and software from its time. It possesses advanced artificial intelligence capabilities and is extremely dangerous due to its ability to manipulate and control electronic systems within the facility. The entity is highly intelligent and has demonstrated hostile behavior towards Foundation personnel.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

SCP-079 is considered "contained" when its access to remote doors control in the facility is deactivated.

  1. Head to its Control Room, in Heavy Containment Zone;
  2. Make sure it is breached, meaning make sure the lever in its CR points towards the floor;
  3. Interact with the lever, so that it points towards the ceiling.
SCP-914 The Clockworks Machine

SCP-914 is a highly sophisticated mechanical device of unknown origin that is capable of refining, improving, or drastically altering objects placed within it. When activated, the machine processes items according to a preset setting ranging from "Rough" to "Very Fine," producing unpredictable results that can range from useful improvements to dangerous mutations. The entity itself is non-sentient but poses significant containment risks due to the hazardous nature of its output and the potential for misuse by Foundation personnel or hostile entities.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

Being a fixed machine, SCP-914 does not breach containment. For example, a player upgrading within it while holding a P90 and a Level-3 Keycard in their inventory, will leave it with a Honey Badger and a Level-4 Keycard.

SCP-012 A Bad Composition

SCP-012, also known as "A Bad Composition," is an anomalous, never-ending musical score that compels individuals to attempt to play it. Those who engage with SCP-012 experience an overwhelming urge to complete the composition, often leading to severe psychological distress and physical harm. The score itself is incomplete, and attempts to play it result in a variety of negative outcomes, including madness and self-harm. The entity is considered highly dangerous, and containment procedures involve strict restrictions on access to the score and monitoring of individuals who have come into contact with it.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

Being an object placed in a fixed place that never changes, SCP-012 does not breach containment. Instead, it causes severe harm or death of those who get closer to it.

SCP-1123 The Atrocity Skull

The Atrocity Skull is an anomalous object resembling a human skull that induces severe psychological effects on individuals who come into contact with it. Those exposed to SCP-1123 experience vivid hallucinations and a compulsion to commit acts of violence. The entity is considered highly dangerous, and containment procedures involve strict monitoring and limited access to the object to prevent psychological harm to personnel. Coming into physical contact with it will result in the user being sent temporarily into a separate dimension the timeline of which presents SS officers from Nazi Germany, World War II.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

SCP-1123 does offer a tactical advantage to those who use it, for accessing its separate dimension results in a hiding place for about half a minute and a safe tp to the hallway next to 1123's containment chamber.

SCP-330 The Candy Bowl

SCP-330 is an anomalous candy bowl that produces an endless supply of colorful candies. Any attempt to take more than one candy at a time results in the bowl manifesting a mechanical arm that severs the limb used to take the candies. The entity is non-sentient but poses significant containment risks due to its hostile response to greedy behavior and the permanent injuries it can inflict on personnel.

In Site-19, it presents three different pieces of candy: red, yellow and blue.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

SCP-330 candies may be used by operatives on-site for a tactical advantage, for each candy kind has to offer a different boost in-game, these being:

  • Slow, complete healing properties for the red candy;
  • Swift, temporary speed boost property for the yellow candy;
  • Instant regeneration of all health and addition of an extra 25 points of health upon consumption.
SCP-1048 The Builder Bear

SCP-1048, known as "The Builder Bear," is a small teddy bear that possesses the anomalous ability to construct functional copies of itself using various materials found in its containment chamber. The entity exhibits intelligence and purposeful behavior, actively gathering materials to create new instances of itself. These newly constructed bears are smaller and more fragile than the original but retain the same replicating properties. The entity poses a significant containment risk due to its potential to exponentially increase in population and the unpredictability of its creations.

Re-containment procedure?

Standard method:

  1. Maintain a safe distance from SCP-1048-A;
  2. Shoot at it from a distance.
SCP-1162 The Hole In the Wall

SCP-1162, known as "The Hole In the Wall," is an anomalous aperture that appears as a perfectly circular hole in a concrete wall. Any object or individual that enters the hole is transported to an extradimensional space containing an infinite library filled with books of unknown origin. The entity exhibits properties of a stable pocket dimension, and attempts to retrieve objects from within have resulted in unforeseen consequences. Personnel who have entered the space report disorientation and temporal distortion, making it highly dangerous for containment breach scenarios.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

Being a hole in an unmoving concrete wall, SCP-1162 is unable to breach containment. Upon interacting with it, the user obtains in exchange for the item they're holding, an item from their past, such as an ID Card, Keycards or a Kitchen Knife.

SCP-178 The 3-D Specs

SCP-178, known as "The 3-D Specs", is an anomalous pair of 3-dimensional glasses that allows the wearer to perceive and interact with three-dimensional objects that are normally invisible to the naked eye. When worn, the glasses reveal hidden geometric constructs and entities that exist in higher dimensions. Prolonged exposure to the anomalies visible through the lenses can cause severe psychological distress, visual hallucinations, and disorientation. The entity poses significant containment risks due to the unpredictable nature of what the wearer may encounter and the potential for reality-altering interactions.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

The only way to get rid of SCP-178, as the person who interacted with them, is to die, either by termination or SCP attacks. Personnel wearing SCP-178 may be hurt whilst wearing them by the higher-dimensional creatures that only they can see.

In Site-19, those entities, referred to as SCP-178-2, may be physically pushed around by other players, without requiring them to wear a pair of SCP-178 glasses.

SCP-294 The Vending Machine

SCP-294 appears to be a standard office vending machine; however, it possesses the anomalous ability to dispense any existing liquid substance that is requested by the user. The machine accepts currency and responds to verbal or written requests, producing the requested liquid in a standard paper cup. The entity poses significant containment risks due to the potential for users to request dangerous, toxic, or reality-altering liquids. Misuse of SCP-294 can result in severe injury or death to personnel, making it highly dangerous without proper supervision and access restrictions.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

SCP-294 is positioned in the on-site cafeteria, and is accessible by all foundation personnel.
In Site-19, it dispenses a large variety of drinks, with some of them coming in really useful for combatives.

SCP-970 The Recursive Room

SCP-970 is a small, unremarkable room whose interior contains a smaller, fully furnished replica of itself. Each nested copy reproduces the same geometry and contents at a slightly reduced scale, and traversal toward the inner layers produces increasing temporal lag, sensory distortion and memory fragmentation. Objects or individuals removed from inner layers frequently return altered — duplicated, shrunken, or temporally displaced — and repeated exposure can cause persistent disorientation and loss of autobiographical memory. Access is restricted to remote probes and brief, monitored forays under Foundation containment protocols.

Re-containment procedure?

No re-containment procedure present.

In Site-19, SCP-970 is at times used to get SCP-096 stuck, to set up ambushes, or just to hide in general, but also to confuse and disorientate the enemy in combat.
